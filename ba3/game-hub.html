Variety Detective — blind-IPA identification

You see the IPA. Not the word. Pick the variety. Three difficulty tiers. Your Elo rating adjusts by how hard the item was — correct guesses on outer-circle items move you up faster; wrong guesses on tier-1 items cost more.

Rule-Application Duel — stopwatch mode

A phonological rule is stated verbatim from the source. You apply it to a word. Streak multipliers: 3 in a row = ×1.5, 6 = ×2, 10 = ×3. A wrong answer or a 20-second timeout breaks the streak.

Rules drill: SVLR (Stuart-Smith 57), yod-dropping (Ihsane 2026 W5a), rhoticity, TRAP-BATH split, T-flapping, TH-stopping, dark /l/ (Bauer 36).

Two-Truths-One-Lie — spot the misstatement

Each card presents three statements about one variety. Two are accurate; one is a canonical exam trap (drawn from the 30-trap catalogue in Derivative-B-Canonical-Traps.md). You have 30 seconds to pick the lie. Hover any statement after the reveal to see the source citation.

How the heatmap works (localStorage schema)

Each cell stores the last 20 1/0 attempts as an array at key ba3-gamehub-v1.attempts[Variety|Feature]. Accuracy is rolling over that window. The Daily Boss auto-selects the cell with the lowest accuracy and at least 3 attempts, breaking ties by least-recently tested.
